Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 21, 2026

Daily Budget App ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you use our mobile application.

1. Information Collection

Our application is designed to prioritize user privacy. For the standard version of the app, we do not collect or store any personal financial data on our servers. All budget entries, income, and expense records are stored locally on your device's secure storage.

2. Use of Information

The data you enter into the application is used solely for the purpose of providing you with personal financial tracking and visualization features. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to outside parties.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party tools to improve app performance and stability (such as Google Firebase for crash reporting). These services may collect information about your device and app usage patterns. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more details.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures within the app to protect the integrity of your local data. However, as the data is stored on your device, we recommend maintaining device-level security (passcodes, biometric locks).

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Your continued use of the application after any changes signifies your acceptance of the updated terms.
